The Map

See where your friends actually go

A living map of every spot your kakis have pinned and rated — not sponsored listings, not random strangers. Just the places your friends genuinely rate.

  • Filter by food, cafe, drinks, chill — even the cleanest toilet in town
  • Toggle between your pins and your friends' pins
  • Tap any pin to peek reviews, photos and the group chat
OpenJios

Jio the squad in two taps

Pick a spot, drop a time, and send an open invite to your friends. No more 200-message group chats just to decide where to makan.

  • One-tap open invites tied to a real spot
  • See who's going and who's a maybe at a glance
  • A chat per spot so the details don't get lost
Ranks & Streaks

Climb the leaderboard

Every spot you pin and review earns points. Keep your streak alive, collect badges, and flex on the group chat with a weekly leaderboard that resets every Sunday.

  • Weekly friend leaderboards — close friends, foodies and more
  • Streaks and collectible badges for every milestone
  • Points for spots, reviews and jios you host
Your profile

Your map, your badges, your flex

Every spot you've pinned and every badge you've earned, all in one profile you can share with a tap. Your taste, mapped.

  • A personal collection of every spot you've pinned
  • Badges like Hawker Hero, Early Bird and Explorer
  • Share your map and add friends with a QR code

Privacy

Your map. Your friends. Your business.

JioLeh is built friends-first. Here's the short version of how we treat your data.

Friends-only by default

Your pins, reviews and jios are visible only to friends you add — never the public, never strangers, never sold to brands.

We don't sell your data

No ad networks, no data brokers, no sponsored pins disguised as friends' picks. The map stays honest.

Location, only when needed

We use your location to show nearby spots while you're on the map. You stay in control and can turn it off anytime.

This is a plain-language summary, not the full policy. The complete Privacy Policy describes exactly what we collect (account details, the spots and reviews you create, and basic usage analytics), how long we keep it, and how to request deletion. Questions? Email kimiyang@u.nus.edu.
